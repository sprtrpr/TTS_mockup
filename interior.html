Employment Training

“We develop people, not just skills.” At the Training Source, we offer a comprehensive 300-hour Office and Professional Services Training Program that helps program participants obtain employment skills that are currently in demand by area employers. These classes are available to eligible unemployed or underemployed citizens at no cost thanks to our generous funders. This program utilizes self-paced instruction which allows each student to develop skills according to his or her learning capabilities. Click here to see our work in action.

Key components of this 300-hour
program include:

Computer Skills
Computer Concepts, Keyboarding/Typing, MS Windows and MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, Publisher, and Outlook)

Life Skills
Self-Esteem and Motivation, Time/Money Management, Business English, and Communications

Professional Skills
Resume Preparation, Business Correspondence, Interview Techniques, Dressing for Success, Successful Job Search Strategies, and Job Survival Tips.

When are Office and Professional Services Training Classes Held?
  • Classes are held Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. for 10 weeks. Call now for the start date of our next Office and Professional Services Training class.
What are the Requirements for Admission?
  • 18 years of age or older
  • High school diploma or GED
  • Possess a desire to work and make improvements in your life
How Much Does the Office and Professional Services Training Program Cost?
  • Program participation is exclusively available for eligible candidates. All candidates admitted into the program receive full funding as qualified Maryland or District of Columbia residents. Additionally, participants receive complimentary job placement and follow-up services after training for up to a full year of service.
Why should you choose The Training Source, Inc?
  • The Training Source, Inc. has provided employment training, individual and group counseling, job preparation and placement assistance and follow-up services since 1993. The Training Source is staffed with caring and committed individuals who are qualified in their respective fields. Hundreds of citizens have successfully completed employment training with The Training Source and gone on to enjoy successful careers in a variety of industries.
